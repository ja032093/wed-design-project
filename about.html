ABOUT AL-AQSA FOOTBALL CLUB



For the past 16 years we have been working in youth development and education programs. Currently We have over 200 youth taking part in our activities such as football training, residential and day trips, both in the winter and summer.


Where we take the kids out from the concrete jungle to experience the country side. We also run workshops and talks throughout the year so that the youth can benefit from it



At the Moment we train once a week every Sunday between 1:30 pm - 3:30 pm and we fund and operate everything ourselves with the support of the parents board commitee.


We aim to expand to a wider community so that more youth can benefit from training and exercise.


AL AQSA is a sub-organisation, our parent company is (HADA) - Horn of Africa Development Association. A registered charity (1108582) dedicated to funding food and shelter to the Eritrean refugees who migrated to the Coast of Sudan and are currently displaced in the desert, many are young orphans.


Al Aqsa trains boys but we have not forgotten about the young females also in the community, so we have just set up an Aqsa Girls Youth Club also every Sunday with fun packed activities and programmes such as Arts & Crafts, Lectures, Cooking, Projects, Sports, Day Trips and Camping etc.
