Al Aqsa FC was established in 2002, we are a community dedicated in motivating youth by encouraging them to participate in sports and outdoor activities.






DONATE

AL AQSA FC operates on contributions from organizations and people like you. Your Donations help us expand to wider communities and build more programmes for more youth.


OUR MISSION


AL AQSA FC wants to raise funds to secure a youth club, a safe and enjoyable environment, a place where the youth can come to and share their troubles and seek advice. It will be a place that would keep them away from the negative influences around them. We as a community, need to raise funds to secure a place so that our work with the youth can continue.

The youth of today are our future, if we don't invest in them now, what kind of future will they have?
